Signs You Should Call Us:

Contact us if you notice any of the following signs in your home or business:

  • Frequent Circuit Breaker Trips
  • Hot Outlets or Switches
  • Burning or Smoky Door
  • Sparking Outlets or Switches
  • Buzzing or Crackling Sounds
  • Electric Shocks
  • Defective Electrical Outlet
  • Dimming or Flickering Lights
  • Very High Electrical Bill

Any other concerns or suspicions with your electrical components.
